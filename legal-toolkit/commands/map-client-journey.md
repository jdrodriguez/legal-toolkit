# /map-client-journey -- Client Journey Mapper

Map how clients move through your firm from first contact through post-case, identifying where the experience breaks down and where you can improve conversion, retention, and reputation.

@$1

## Workflow

- **Gather inputs**: practice area, current process description, client feedback (if available), firm size/roles, and tools in use
- **Map the journey** stage by stage with activities, roles, client emotions, pain points, and drop-off risks
- **Analyze pain points** across all stages, ranked by impact on clients and the firm
- **Identify drop-off points** at key stage transitions with estimated revenue impact
- **Find automation opportunities** that reduce friction without losing the human touch
- **Surface coaching gaps** where staff training or behavior needs improvement
- **Build a roadmap** of prioritized improvements grouped into quick wins, 30-day, and 90-day timelines
- Refer to the `map-client-journey` skill (SKILL.md) for journey stages, practice-area adaptations, and analysis frameworks
