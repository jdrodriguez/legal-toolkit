# /design-comm-cadence -- Communication Cadence Designer

Design a structured communication calendar that keeps clients informed, reduces anxiety-driven calls, and builds trust throughout the case lifecycle. Produces a 90-day touchpoint schedule, ready-to-use message templates, cadence adjustments by client anxiety level, and automation recommendations.

@$1

## Workflow

- **Gather inputs**: practice area/case type, estimated timeline, preferred contact frequency, available channels, client anxiety level, and case complexity
- **Generate calendar**: build a 90-day communication calendar with specific touchpoints, channels, responsible parties, and purpose for each entry
- **Draft templates**: produce 10 message templates covering welcome, status updates, hearing prep, results, milestones, check-ins, and case resolution
- **Recommend automation**: identify which touchpoints to automate, keep personal, or handle as hybrid
- **Define escalation protocol**: specify when staff should escalate to the attorney
- Refer to the `design-comm-cadence` skill (SKILL.md) for cadence strategies, touchpoint types, template standards, and edge case handling
